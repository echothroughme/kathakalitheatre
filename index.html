About Us

Thiranottam Kathakali Theatre is one of the organization working in the field of Art, Culture, and especially for Kathakali. For the Preservation, promotion and propagation of our rich culture, tradition and heritage through ethnic performing arts of Kerala. We start in the beginning of 2010's but officially in 2020, the founder of thiranottam kathakali theatre was Kalanilayam Sandeep. He did hundred of stage performence, tours and festivels in all over india.

Services

  • We do training and workshops for local and international students.

  • We do stage perfomence (Kathakali,Koodiyattam,Bharatanatyam,etc.. and also we do special programmes for our guest request),

  • We participate Local and International events, tours, cultural programmes and festivels all over the world.
